Hello, my name is Kennedy. I am an award-winning interactive designer with a keen eye for details while keeping a clear vision of the big picture. I am a founding partner of Theron Studios, Status Chart, Grade Spotter, and Kenvak. I have also worked with powerful brands such as Google, Wine Spectator, Porsche, Coca-Cola, Spotify, and MLB. My main focus is designing mobile, tablet, and wearable apps, however I have designed and launched several successful and engaging websites over the past ten years.

During my off hours I am very active in the startup, hackathon, and New York tech scene. In 2012 my team PoachBase won second place at TechCrunch Disrupt in NYC, one of the world's largest hackathons. While on the 2011 StartupBus I was nicknamed "The Machine" for my hard work and dedication. I truly love what I do. There is always something exciting to learn and opportunities to help refine the craft. Outside of work I love surfing, swimming, running, turning wrenches, and spending time with my wife.

The history

The original Kennedy’s Garage was opened by my grandfather Theron Kennedy, in Lake of the Ozark, Missouri on February 28th, 1941. Although 1941 was a different time and the business was building cars, nothing has changed as far as working hard. The key to his success was the long term relationships that he developed with his customers and the community.

Today I still value those traditions and enjoy the old fashioned handshake. The original Kennedy’s Garage was shut down after the passing of Theron. I later reclaimed the name and wanted to keep the same values of my grandfather. Since then the family has moved away from building cars, but we still have a deep passion for them.
