About Me

Hello, my name is Kennedy and I am an entrepreneur. I currently build websites, and I am loving every minute of it. I live in the huge city of New York with my wife Sara and two morbidly obese cats. I stand tall at 5' 18" and I am ambidextrous. I grew up in Kansas City, MO and then later moved to Flagler Beach, FL. I grew up with a passion for the arts. I loved my art and photography classes and doodled in all the others. When I am not being a geek I enjoy surfing, swimming, skateboarding, drifting with my kid brother, and traveling.

The History

The original Kennedy’s Garage was open by Theron Kennedy, in Lake of the Ozark, Missouri on February 29th, 1941. Granted 1941 was a different time and the business was building cars, nothing has changed as far as working hard. The key to his success was the relationships that he developed with his customers and the community.

Today I still value those traditions and enjoy the old fashion handshake. The original Kennedy’s Garage was shut down after the passing of Theron. I later reopened the name and wanted to keep the same values of my grandfather. Since then the family has moved away from building cars, we still have a deep passion for them, but now I build amazing ideas surrounding the internet.
